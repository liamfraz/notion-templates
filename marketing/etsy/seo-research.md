### Key Observations

- **Construction niche is wide open.** Almost no quality Notion construction templates on Etsy. SiteOS can dominate this niche with minimal competition.

- **"Professional" beats "aesthetic"** for FlowTech's audience. Most Etsy Notion templates target students/lifestyle. Professional positioning reduces competition.
- **Bundle listings perform well** on Etsy because the per-item value perception is strong. Always cross-link individual listings to the bundle.

## Listing Optimisation Schedule

### Renewal Strategy

Etsy charges $0.20 USD per listing (or renewal). Renewing bumps your listing in search results — it's the cheapest advertising on the platform.

| Action | Frequency | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Renew all 5 listings | Every 2-3 months | $1.00 USD | Search recency boost. Etsy favours recently listed/renewed items |
| Renew best performer | Monthly | $0.20 USD | Keep your top seller visible. Check stats to identify it |
| Seasonal refresh (titles/tags) | Quarterly | Free | Swap 2-3 tags to match seasonal search trends (e.g., "new year planning" in Jan) |
| Full listing audit | Every 6 months | Free | Review conversion rates. Rewrite underperforming descriptions |

### Seasonal Tag Swaps

| Season | Swap In | Swap Out |
|---|---|---|
| Jan-Feb | "new year planner", "goal tracker" | Lower-performing generic tags |
| Mar-Apr | "Q2 planning", "business planning" | Seasonal tags from Q1 |
| Sep-Oct | "Q4 planning", "year-end review" | Summer seasonal tags |
| Nov-Dec | "gift for developer", "digital gift" | Lowest-performing tags |

## Best Times to List on Etsy

Etsy search gives a small boost to newly listed items. Timing your initial listing (and renewals) maximises this window.

| Factor | Recommendation | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Day of week | Tuesday-Thursday | Highest Etsy traffic for digital products. Avoid weekends (craft/handmade buyers dominate) |
| Time of day | 9-11 AM EST (US East Coast) | US buyers are the largest Etsy market. Morning = full day of visibility |
| Australian time | 12-2 AM AEDT (midnight-2am) | Corresponds to US morning. Schedule listings the night before |
| Avoid | Major US holidays, Etsy-wide sales events | Your listing gets buried under promoted/sale items |

### Launch Sequence

1. **List SiteOS first** — lowest competition niche, likely to get traction fastest
2. **List Consultant OS second** — "freelance" and "CRM" terms have good search volume with moderate competition
3. **List PM OS third** — high competition but strong product. Needs reviews to rank
4. **List AgentOps fourth** — emerging niche. Will grow over time
5. **List Bundle last** — needs individual listings live first so you can cross-link

Space listings 1-2 days apart. Each new listing gets a "new listing" boost — don't waste them all on the same day.

## Etsy-Specific Tips for Digital Products

### Pricing Psychology
- Etsy buyers expect lower prices than Gumroad for digital products
- Consider AUD→USD conversion: $99 AUD ≈ $64 USD for SiteOS
- List prices in USD (Etsy auto-converts for international buyers)
- Use .99 pricing on Etsy (e.g., $28.99 instead of $29) — convention on the platform

### Reviews Drive Everything
- First 5 reviews are critical. Consider reaching out to existing Gumroad buyers for cross-platform reviews
- Include a "Leave a Review" card in your digital download ZIP
- Respond to every review within 24 hours

### Etsy Ads (Optional)
- Start with $1-5/day budget on your best-performing listing only
- Etsy Ads work best for low-competition niches (SiteOS, AgentOps)
- Don't run ads until you have at least 3 reviews — conversion rate is too low without social proof

### Cross-Linking
- Every individual listing description should mention the bundle
- Bundle description should link to individual listings
- Use Etsy's "related listings" feature to connect all 5 listings
