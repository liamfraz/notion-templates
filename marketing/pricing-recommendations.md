# Pricing Recommendations

## Summary

| Template | Price | Currency | Rationale |
|----------|-------|----------|-----------|
| AgentOps | $29 (intro) → $49 | AUD | Niche audience, newer category. Low entry to build reviews |
| PM OS | $39 | USD | Broadest market. USD for international reach. Mid-range for competitive space |
| Consultant OS | $49 | AUD | High-value audience (consultants billing $150-350/hr). Easy ROI argument |
| SiteOS | $99 | AUD | Niche industry, high pain, no Notion alternatives. Premium justified |
| Bundle | $129 | AUD | 40% discount on $216 combined. Strong anchor effect |

## Pricing Logic

### AgentOps — $29 AUD (intro) → $49 AUD

**Why $29 intro:**
- New product category — "AI ops dashboard" isn't an established Gumroad search term yet
- Lower price reduces purchase friction for a template buyers haven't seen before
- 7 databases + 10 starter prompts + code review checklist justifies $29 easily
- Rising price creates urgency ("$29 now, $49 after 100 sales")

**Why AUD:**
- Primary audience includes Australian developers (your network)
- AUD appears cheaper to USD buyers (~$19 USD equivalent)

**Comparable Gumroad products:** Most AI/developer Notion templates sell for $15-39 USD.

---

### PM OS — $39 USD

**Why $39:**
- Competitive PM template market — prices range from $19-79 USD
- 8 connected databases + 3 dashboards + risk register + decision log puts this in the upper tier
- $39 is the sweet spot: high enough to signal quality, low enough for impulse buy
- Risk register + decision log are differentiators that justify premium over $19-29 competitors

**Why USD:**
- Broadest target audience (PMs globally)
- USD is the standard for Gumroad templates
- "Lifetime updates" adds perceived value without ongoing cost

**Comparable:** Thomas Frank's PM templates sell for $49-79. Simpler PM templates sell for $15-29.

---

### Consultant OS — $49 AUD

**Why $49:**
- Target audience bills at $150-350/hr AUD — $49 is 10-20 minutes of billable time
- SOW template alone saves 2-4 hours per proposal (worth $300-1,400 in billable time)
- 10 interconnected systems (CRM, pipeline, projects, time, invoices, rate card, KB, skills, dashboard, review)
- Higher price signals "this is a business tool, not a personal planner"

**Why AUD:**
- Australian consulting rates and sample data
- AUD pricing (~$32 USD) still feels cheap to international buyers

**Comparable:** Consulting/freelance Notion templates: $29-69 USD. Business-in-a-box templates: $49-99.

---

### SiteOS — $99 AUD

**Why $99:**
- Zero direct competition — no construction-specific Notion templates exist at this quality
- Replaces software costing $5,000-50,000+/year (Procore, Aconex, PlanGrid)
- 9 connected databases covering the full construction PM workflow
- Niche audience with high willingness to pay (construction companies have budgets)
- Sample data is industry-specific and realistic — can't be replicated by generic templates

**Why AUD:**
- Australian construction industry is the primary market
- Construction terminology and workflows are Australian-specific
- $99 AUD (~$65 USD) is still nothing compared to construction software

**Comparable:** No direct comparables. Closest: industry-specific Notion templates sell for $49-149 USD.

---

### Bundle — $129 AUD

**Why $129:**
- Individual total: $29 + $49 + $39 USD (~$59 AUD) + $99 = $236 AUD equivalent
- Simplified to $216 AUD for marketing (using listed prices)
- 40% discount ($129 vs $216) is a strong psychological anchor
- Buyers who want 2+ templates will always pick the bundle at this price
- Average order value increases significantly

**Why it works:**
- Bundle price is only $30 more than SiteOS alone — makes the bundle a no-brainer for anyone considering SiteOS
- For PM OS buyers, they get 3 extra templates for $90 more
- Positions FlowTech as a "suite" rather than individual products

---

## Price Increase Strategy

1. **Launch week:** All products at listed prices + `LAUNCH20` discount code (20% off)
2. **After 50 sales per product:** Remove the LAUNCH20 code
3. **After 100 AgentOps sales:** Raise to $49 AUD (honour the promise in the listing)
4. **After 6 months / 200+ total sales:** Consider raising PM OS to $49 USD and Consultant OS to $59 AUD
5. **Never discount below launch price** — erodes trust with early buyers
