## `// APPLICATIONS TRACKER`

Track every role you apply to. No more losing track of where you stand.

### How to Use

For every application you submit, add a row with:
- Company name and role title
- Where you found it (LinkedIn, referral, etc.)
- Current status (Applied, Screening, Interview, etc.)
- Priority level (Dream Job, Strong Fit, Good Option, Safety Net)
- Your salary expectations for the role

### Status Definitions

```
Applied     = Application submitted, waiting for response
Screening   = Recruiter reviewing your application
Phone Screen = Initial call with recruiter or hiring manager
Technical   = Technical interview round(s)
Onsite      = Final round / onsite visit
Offer       = Offer received
Rejected    = They said no
Withdrawn   = You pulled out
Ghosted     = No response after 3+ weeks
```

### Quick Research Checklist

```
Before applying to any company:
- [ ] Read their Glassdoor reviews (focus on engineering-specific)
- [ ] Check their engineering blog or tech talks
- [ ] Look for recent news (funding, layoffs, product launches)
- [ ] Find 1-2 people in your network who work there
- [ ] Verify the salary range is in your acceptable band
```

### Networking Best Practices

```
DO:
- Be specific about what you're looking for
- Offer value before asking for help
- Follow up within 48 hours of any meeting
- Keep people updated on your progress
- Thank everyone who helps, regardless of outcome

DON'T:
- Send generic "I'm looking for a job" messages
- Ask for a referral in the first message
- Ghost people who helped you after you land a role
- Only reach out when you need something
```
