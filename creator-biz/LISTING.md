### What's inside

**Income Streams Tracker** — Every revenue source mapped with monthly revenue, expenses, net profit, and effort hours. See which streams are worth your time and which are costing you.

**Brand Deals Pipeline** — Track deals from cold lead to paid invoice. Kanban board, contact details, deliverables, deadlines, and payment terms. Never forget to follow up or invoice.

**Digital Products Manager** — Revenue, units sold, platform, and status for everything you sell. See your product ladder and identify what to build next.

**Business Dashboard** — Revenue, expenses, net profit, tax provisions, and pipeline value at a glance. Updated monthly.

**Negotiation Playbook** — Rate cards by audience size, CPM formulas, and a negotiation framework for brand deals.

**Quarterly Business Review** — A structured 2-hour review covering revenue, expenses, deals, products, and goals. With checklists.

## FAQ

**Q: Is this a content calendar?**
A: No. This is the business and finance side of being a creator. If you need a content calendar, check out the Content Creator Command Center. This template pairs perfectly with it — one handles content, the other handles money.

**Q: Can I import this into an existing Notion workspace?**
A: Yes. Import the main template as a new page and the CSVs as databases. Works alongside your existing setup without conflicts.

**Q: Do I need a paid Notion plan?**
A: The free plan works for all features. A paid plan gives you unlimited file uploads and better database properties.

**Q: Is this only for Australian creators?**
A: No. The tax section mentions Australian ABN/GST because the creator is Australian, but all advice is globally applicable. The expense categories, income tracking, and business frameworks work for creators in any country.

**Q: How is this different from a spreadsheet?**
A: Notion databases give you multiple views (table, board, calendar), relations between databases (link a deal to its invoice and its CRM contact), rollups (auto-calculate totals from related records), and formulas. A spreadsheet can do the maths but cannot give you a Kanban pipeline for brand deals.

**Q: Do you offer support?**
A: The setup guide covers everything including troubleshooting. If you are stuck, reach out via the contact details on the product page.
