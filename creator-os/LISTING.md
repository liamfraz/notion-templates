## Description

### Your content deserves a system. Not chaos.

You're creating for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, newsletters, and three other platforms. Your ideas live in Notes, your brand deals in email, your analytics in six different dashboards, and your income tracking in a spreadsheet you last updated two months ago.

Sound familiar?

**Content Creator Command Center** is a Notion workspace built specifically for multi-platform content creators. Not a generic project manager with a content label slapped on it — this one understands the creator workflow from idea to income.

### What's inside

**Content Calendar** — Every piece of content across every platform in one view. Board, calendar, and table views. Drag content through stages from Idea to Published. See your entire week, month, and pipeline at a glance.

**Analytics Tracker** — Log views, engagement, shares, and revenue for everything you publish. Performance ratings from Viral to Flop. Know what's working and do more of it.

**Brand Deals Pipeline** — Track sponsorships from first pitch to final payment. Fee tracking, deliverables, deadlines, and payment status. Never forget to invoice a brand again.

**Income Dashboard** — Every dollar tracked by source: ad revenue, sponsorships, affiliates, digital products, coaching, memberships. See your revenue diversification and monthly trends.

**Platform Manager** — All your platforms in one database. Follower counts, growth rates, posting frequency, and priority levels. Cross-platform repurposing workflow included.

**Idea Bank** — Capture ideas from audience requests, trends, collaborations, and personal experiences. Score and prioritise so you always know what to create next.

**Monthly Creator Review** — A structured 90-minute review template covering income, analytics, growth, and goal-setting. Stop guessing and start measuring.

**Creator Dashboard** — Revenue, growth, engagement, and pipeline value at a glance.
