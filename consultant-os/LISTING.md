## Description

### Stop duct-taping your consulting business together.

You're great at building systems for your clients. But your own business runs on scattered spreadsheets, forgotten follow-ups, and invoices you send two weeks late.

**Technical Consultant OS** is a Notion workspace template built specifically for IT consultants, freelance developers, and automation specialists. Not another generic freelancer template — this one understands that you deliver technical projects, not just bill hours.

### What's inside

**Client CRM** — Every client in one place with engagement history, health scoring, and relationship tracking. Know who needs attention before they have to chase you.

**Lead Pipeline** — Track prospects from first contact to signed deal. See your weighted pipeline value, conversion rates, and where each opportunity stands.

**Project Tracker** — Active projects with milestones, deliverables, risk registers, and budget tracking. Know when a project is going off the rails before it's too late.

**SOW & Proposal Templates** — Professional Statement of Work templates you can customise in minutes, not hours. Includes a fully worked example for a real automation project.

**Rate Card Manager** — Manage standard, discounted, and premium rates across all your service types. Stop second-guessing what to charge.

**Time Tracker** — Log hours by client, project, and category. Auto-calculate billable amounts. Track your utilisation rate to make sure you're not leaving money on the table.

**Invoice Register** — Track every invoice from draft to paid. Spot overdue invoices instantly. Follow a proven chase process so you actually get paid.

**Knowledge Base** — Store reusable deliverables, code snippets, checklists, and documentation templates. Stop rebuilding the same thing for every client.

**Skills Matrix** — Track your technical skills, certifications, and learning goals. Know what's expiring and where to invest your development time.

**Monthly Business Review** — A structured template to review your business health on the first of every month. Revenue, utilisation, pipeline, client health — all in one review.

**Business Dashboard** — Revenue, active projects, pipeline value, and utilisation rate at a glance.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I import this into an existing Notion workspace?**
A: Yes. Import the main template as a new page and the CSVs as databases. It works alongside your existing setup.

**Q: Do I need Notion paid plan?**
A: The free Notion plan works, but a paid plan gives you unlimited file uploads and better database features.

**Q: Is the sample data realistic?**
A: Yes. All sample data uses Australian consulting scenarios with realistic rates ($150-350/hr AUD), client types, and project structures.

**Q: Can I customise the databases?**
A: Absolutely. Add columns, change select options, create new views — it's your workspace. The setup guide explains how everything connects.

**Q: What if I'm not in Australia?**
A: The template works globally. Just update the currency in your number columns and adjust the rate card to your market.

**Q: Do you offer support?**
A: The setup guide covers everything including troubleshooting. If you're stuck, reach out via the contact details on the product page.
