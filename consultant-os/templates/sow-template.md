## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Background

Meridian Property Group currently processes approximately 200 expense claims per month using a paper-based system. Staff complete PDF forms, attach receipts, print and hand-deliver to their manager for approval, who then forwards to the finance team for data entry into the accounting system.

This process takes an average of 12 business days from submission to reimbursement. Common issues include lost receipts, illegible forms, routing to the wrong approver, and manual data entry errors. The finance team spends approximately 15 hours per week on expense claim processing.

Meridian has engaged FlowTech Consulting to design and implement an automated expense claims workflow using Microsoft Power Automate and SharePoint Online, which are already licensed under Meridian's existing Microsoft 365 E3 agreement.

### 1.2 Objectives

The objective of this engagement is to:

- Eliminate paper-based expense claim processing entirely
- Reduce average processing time from 12 business days to 3 business days
- Reduce finance team effort on expense processing by 80%

### 1.3 Success Criteria

This project will be considered successful when:

- [ ] Staff can submit expense claims digitally from any device
- [ ] Claims are automatically routed to the correct approver based on org hierarchy
- [ ] Approved claims are automatically exported to the accounting system
- [ ] Processing time is reduced to 3 business days or less (measured over first full month)
- [ ] Finance team confirms effort reduction of at least 60%

---

## 2. Scope of Work

### 2.1 In Scope

1. **Digital Expense Claim Form** — Power Apps canvas app for mobile and desktop. Supports receipt photo upload, expense categories, project codes, and multi-line claims.
2. **Approval Workflow** — Power Automate cloud flow with multi-level approval (manager then finance), delegation rules for absent approvers, and automatic escalation after 3 business days.
3. **Accounting Integration** — Automated export of approved claims to Meridian's Xero accounting system via API.
4. **Reporting Dashboard** — Power BI dashboard showing claim volumes, processing times, spend by category, and approval bottlenecks.
5. **User Training** — Two 1-hour training sessions (one for staff, one for finance/approvers) plus quick reference guides.

### 2.2 Out of Scope

- Changes to Meridian's organisational hierarchy or approval policy (consultant will implement the current policy as-is)
- Credit card reconciliation or corporate card integration
- Historical data migration (past expense claims will remain in existing system)
- Ongoing support beyond the 2-week warranty period
