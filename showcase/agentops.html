Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About AgentOps

Can I customize the databases?
Yes, every database is fully customizable. Add columns, change categories, adjust views, and tailor everything to your workflow. It's your Notion workspace — make it yours.

Does it integrate with AI APIs automatically?
Not out of the box — it's a Notion template, so data entry is manual by default. However, the setup guide includes ideas for automating data entry with n8n or Power Automate webhooks to push session data directly into your databases.

What Notion plan do I need?
AgentOps works on any Notion plan, including the free plan. No paid Notion subscription required.

Is there a free version?
Yes, AgentOps Lite is completely free and includes the AI Session Logger, Prompt Library, and a basic cost tracker. It's a great way to try the system before upgrading to Premium.

Can I use this for a team?
Yes, share your Notion workspace and use the Owner field to assign items to team members. Works well for teams of 2–10 developers working with AI tools.
