FAQ

Common Questions

What types of construction projects does this work for?
Commercial, residential multi-storey, institutional, and civil/infrastructure with minor customisation. The databases and workflows are designed around the standard Australian construction PM workflow, but the structure translates across project types.
What Notion plan do I need?
Works on any Notion plan including the free plan. No premium Notion features are required.
Is the sample data realistic?
Yes, realistic Australian construction scenarios across all 9 databases. Projects, defects, RFIs, variations, and more are populated with data that mirrors real-world construction projects so you can see exactly how the template works before adding your own data.
Can I use this on my phone on site?
Yes, Notion works on desktop and mobile. Log defects and diary entries from your phone while you walk the site. The database views are optimised for both screen sizes.
Does this replace Procore?
For small-mid builders managing 1–5 projects, it covers the essentials at a fraction of the cost. Not suited for enterprise BIM integration or formal transmittal workflows. If you need full document control with formal transmittals, Procore or Aconex is the right choice. If you need a practical, affordable system that covers 80% of what a construction PM does daily, SiteOS is built for you.
