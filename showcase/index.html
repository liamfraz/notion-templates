Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need a paid Notion plan?

All templates work on Notion's free plan. Every database, linked view, and formula works on every tier. A paid plan gives you unlimited file uploads and better sharing — nice to have, not required.

How long does setup take?

Under an hour for any template. Each one includes a step-by-step Setup Guide with screenshots. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can set this up. You duplicate the template, import the CSV files, connect the database relations, then replace the sample data with your own.

Can I customise the databases?

Completely. Add columns, change categories, rename properties, create new views. The templates give you a proven starting structure — make it yours. The Setup Guide explains how everything connects so you know what you can safely change.

Can I use these with my team?

Yes. Share the Notion workspace with your team and use the owner/assignee fields to filter views per person. Works well for teams of 2–10. Notion handles permissions — one purchase covers your whole team.

What's the difference between Lite and Premium?

Every template includes a free Lite version — typically 3 core databases to get you started immediately. Premium adds the full database suite, dashboards, automation recipes, formula library, and realistic sample data. Lite is a genuine working tool, not a teaser. Premium is what you use when the workflow becomes central to your day.

What if I'm not satisfied?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If the template doesn't work for your workflow, just request a refund via Gumroad within 30 days of purchase.

Do prices include updates?

Yes. One-time purchase, lifetime updates. When templates are improved or extended, existing buyers get access to the updated files via Gumroad at no extra cost.

Can I import these into an existing Notion workspace?

Yes. Import the main template as a new page in your workspace and import the CSV files as databases. Each template is self-contained and works alongside your existing setup without disrupting anything.
