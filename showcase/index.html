FAQ

Common Questions

What Notion plan do I need?
All templates work on any Notion plan, including Free. Databases, relations, and linked views work on all tiers. A paid plan gives you unlimited file uploads and more advanced features.
How do I set it up?
Duplicate the template to your Notion workspace, import the CSV files for sample data, and follow the included step-by-step Setup Guide. The whole process takes under an hour — no technical knowledge required.
Can I customise the databases?
Absolutely. Add columns, change categories, create new views, adjust formulas — it’s your workspace. The sample data gives you a starting point; replace it with your own data when ready.
Is there a free version?
Yes! Every template includes a free Lite version with core databases. Try it out, and upgrade to Premium when you’re ready for the full system.
Can I use these for a team?
Yes. Share the workspace with your team and Notion handles permissions. No per-user fees — one purchase covers unlimited team members.
What’s your refund policy?
30-day money-back guarantee on all templates. No questions asked. If it’s not the right fit, you get a full refund.
Do templates integrate with other tools automatically?
These are Notion templates, not SaaS products. However, the setup guides include ideas for automating data entry using n8n, Power Automate, or Zapier webhooks if you want to connect external tools.
