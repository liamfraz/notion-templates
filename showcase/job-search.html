Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Can I customize the databases?
Yes, every database is fully customizable. Add columns for specific industries, adjust interview stages, and tailor everything to your job search process.

Is the salary data accurate?
The sample salary data is AU-focused and based on 2026 market research. You can update it with your own research from Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, or recruiter conversations. The database structure is the real value — populate it with data relevant to your target roles.

What Notion plan do I need?
Job Search CRM works on any Notion plan, including the free plan. No paid Notion subscription required.

How long does setup take?
Most people are fully set up within 20 minutes. The setup guide walks you through importing the template and customizing it for your target roles and industries.

Does this work alongside LinkedIn?
Yes, this is designed to complement LinkedIn, not replace it. Use LinkedIn for networking and discovery, then log everything in your CRM to track follow-ups, outcomes, and patterns across your entire job search.
