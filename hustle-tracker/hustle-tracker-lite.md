## `// SIDE HUSTLES`

Track every income stream in one place. Know where your money comes from and how much effort each hustle takes.

### How to Use

Add a row for each side hustle with:
- What it is and the category (E-commerce, Freelance, Content Creation, etc.)
- Which platform you operate on
- Monthly revenue and expenses
- Hours per week invested
- Current status (Active, Scaling, Paused, Planning)

### Quick Revenue Health Check

```
For each hustle, calculate your effective hourly rate:
Monthly Net Profit / (Hours per week x 4.3) = $/hour

Under $25/hour = Red flag. Improve pricing, cut costs, or consider killing it.
$25-$50/hour  = Acceptable. Look for efficiency gains.
$50-$80/hour  = Solid. Protect this time.
Over $80/hour = Premium. Double down and scale.
```
