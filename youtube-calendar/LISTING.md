## Description

### Your YouTube channel deserves a system. Not a generic content calendar.

You're juggling YouTube Studio, spreadsheets, note apps, and mental to-do lists. Your video ideas live in five different places. You have no idea which thumbnail style actually performs best. And your revenue tracking is whatever YouTube Studio shows you -- with no breakdown by source.

Sound familiar?

**YouTube Command Center** is a Notion workspace built exclusively for YouTubers. Not a generic content calendar with "YouTube" in the title -- this one understands the YouTube-specific workflow from video idea to revenue tracking.

### What's inside

**Videos Database** -- Your complete production pipeline. Track every video through 8 stages: Idea, Scripting, Filming, Editing, Thumbnail, Scheduled, Published, Evergreen. Board view lets you drag videos through production. Calendar view shows your upload schedule.

**Analytics Tracker** -- YouTube-specific metrics for every published video. Views, watch hours, average view duration, CTR, impressions, subscribers gained, and revenue. Performance ratings from Viral to Flop so you know exactly what's working.

**Thumbnail A/B Tracker** -- Track every thumbnail version with style tags, CTR data, and impressions. Built-in A/B testing workflow to systematically improve your click-through rate over time.

**Playlist Strategy** -- Manage your playlists as a growth strategy, not an afterthought. Track video counts, total views, and playlist strategy type (Evergreen, Series, Seasonal, Topical).

**Revenue Dashboard** -- Every dollar tracked by source: AdSense, memberships, Super Chats, merch shelf, affiliates, sponsorships. See your revenue diversification and spot over-reliance on any single source.

**Idea Pipeline with SEO Scoring** -- Capture video ideas and evaluate them before committing production time. Score each idea by SEO potential, competition level, and search volume. Always know what to create next.
