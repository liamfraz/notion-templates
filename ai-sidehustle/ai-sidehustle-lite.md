### Quick Revenue Health Check

```
For each project, calculate your effective hourly rate:
Monthly Revenue / Monthly Hours = $/hour

Under $30/hour = Red flag. Automate, outsource, or kill it.
$30-$70/hour  = Acceptable. Look for ways to improve.
Over $70/hour = Strong. Protect this project and scale it.
```

### Tax Reminder (Australia)

```
If you're earning side hustle income:
- Declare ALL income in your tax return
- Register for an ABN as a sole trader
- If turnover exceeds $75K, register for GST
- Keep records for 5 years (ATO requirement)
```
