### What is AI Side Hustle Tracker?

AI Side Hustle Tracker is a Notion template built for solopreneurs running AI-powered side projects -- Gumroad products, Substack newsletters, automation services, AI freelancing, course creation, and more.

It's not a generic budget planner. It's a complete business operations dashboard for people juggling multiple revenue streams and trying to figure out what's actually worth their time.

### What's Inside

**Projects Dashboard**
Track every side hustle in one place. See revenue, status, platform, AI tools used, and your effective hourly rate. Know instantly which projects are worth doubling down on and which to kill.

**Income Log**
Record every dollar that comes in. Track by project, source, and payment method. See your revenue grow (or stall) month over month. Australian tax fields built in -- tax withheld and net amount tracked per entry.

**Expense Tracker**
Log business expenses with tax-deductible flags, receipt tracking, and project allocation. Know exactly what you're spending to run each project. Built for BAS time -- categorised and ready to hand to your accountant.

**Task Board**
Kanban-style task management tied to your projects. Prioritise what matters, track estimated vs actual hours, and stop context-switching between five different to-do apps.

**Launch Checklist**
Repeatable checklist for launching new AI side projects. Covers research, build, pre-launch, launch, and post-launch phases. Never forget a step again.

**Monthly P&L**
Profit and loss snapshot pulling from your income and expenses. See the real numbers -- not just revenue, but actual profit after costs.

**Q: Can I customise the databases?**
A: Absolutely. The CSVs give you a starting point -- add columns, change categories, adjust to your workflow. It's your workspace.

A: Not out of the box -- it's a Notion template, not a SaaS product. But the setup guide includes tips for automating data entry with n8n or Zapier webhooks.

**Q: Is there a free version?**
A: Yes. AI Side Hustle Lite includes the Projects database, Income Log, and a basic Task Board. It's included as a separate file in the download.

**Q: What Notion plan do I need?**
A: Works on any Notion plan, including free. Databases and linked views work on all tiers.

**Q: Is this suitable for Australian tax purposes?**
A: The expense tracker includes tax-deductible flags and the income log tracks tax withheld. It's designed to make BAS time easier, but it's not a substitute for proper accounting software. Hand the exports to your accountant.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: About 15 minutes. Import the markdown, create databases from the CSVs, and you're running.
